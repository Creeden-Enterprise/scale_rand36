# scale_rand36 — RAND 36-Item Health Survey 1.0

A 36-item patient-reported survey measuring 8 health domains; the freely-licensed sibling of the SF-36 (identical items, RAND scoring). SCAFFOLD: the item set is not yet ingested — no content claim until it lands.

- **Instrument:** RAND-36 (RAND-36) · Quality of Life — General
- **Data-pending scaffold** — no item content.
- **Scoring:** Scores 0–100 per subscale; higher = better health. RAND scoring differs from the licensed SF-36 scoring.

## Rights

RAND grants permission to use the RAND 36-Item Health Survey without further written permission, under conditions including a **required credit line** acknowledging that it was developed at RAND as part of the Medical Outcomes Study, and clear identification of any changes.
